Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Personal details provided during booking (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information processed securely through our payment provider
  • Stay preferences and special requests
  • Communications sent to us via email or contact forms

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage your reservation
  • To communicate updates, confirmations, and service information
  • To improve our services and personalise your experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties
  • Data may be shared with payment processors and booking platforms solely to fulfil your reservation
  • We may disclose information if required by law

Data Retention

  • Guest records are retained for up to 5 years for accounting and legal purposes
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • You may opt out of marketing communications at any time

Oscar Hotel

Hotel Policy

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in: from 2:00 PM
  • Check-out: by 23:00 PM (noon)
  • Early check-in and late check-out available upon request, subject to availability
  • A valid photo ID and credit card are required at check-in

Cancellation Policy and Refund

  • Free cancellation up to 21 days before arrival
  • Cancellations between 21-7 days before arrival are subject to a 50% charge of the deposit
  • Cancellation within 7 days of arrival are not legitimate for refunds
  • No-shows will be charged the full booking amount
  • Non-refundable rates are charged in full at the time of booking

Payment & Deposit

  • We accept Visa, Mastercard, and cash
  • All rates are quoted in USD and include applicable taxes

Child Policy

  • Children of all ages are welcome
  • Children under 5 stay free when using existing bedding
  • Extra beds or cribs available on request (additional charge may apply)
  • Children must be supervised in all public areas

Pet Policy

  • Pets are permitted on the premises
  • Please contact us in advance to arrange accommodation for service animals

Smoking Policy

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited in all rooms, and indoor areas
  • A designated smoking area is available next to the main entrance
  • A cleaning fee of 250euros will be charged for smoking violations in rooms
